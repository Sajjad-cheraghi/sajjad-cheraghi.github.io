Educational Explainer – Mehr Petrochemical Co.

Project Type: Corporate Educational Animation
Role: Concept, Design, Animation & Compositing (Solo Production)

This animated explainer video was produced for Mehr Petrochemical Company to raise awareness about safety and best practices in the workplace. I handled the full production pipeline — from the initial idea and script structure to the final animation and compositing.

The animation was done in a clean and modern flat style, tailored for internal training and easy comprehension. All characters and scenes were custom designed to match the client’s visual identity and message goals.
