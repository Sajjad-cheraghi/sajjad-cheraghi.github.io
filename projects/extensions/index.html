LiveView Extension for Adobe CC

Project Type: Adobe Extension Development
Role: Full Development & Integration

I developed LiveView, a smart and lightweight extension for Adobe Creative Cloud, originally commissioned by my friend Navid. This tool provides real-time HTML previews and efficient asset management for motion graphic packages and templates.

As a daily user myself, I optimized the extension to streamline preview workflows inside After Effects and Premiere Pro. Today, LiveView is powered by a fast web-based core and is publicly available for free to the Adobe creative community.
